Learn How to Identify Mushrooms:

Before we proceed with the form, let's look at some important mushroom characteristics that help in identifying whether it's safe to eat or poisonous.

Cap Shape

The shape of the mushroom cap can be bell-shaped, conical, or flat. This helps us identify the type of mushroom.

Cap Surface

The surface of the cap can be smooth, scaly, or fibrous. This is another characteristic used to distinguish between different types of mushrooms.

Odor

The odor of a mushroom can vary. Some mushrooms may have a sweet smell, while others may smell like almonds or even unpleasant odors.
